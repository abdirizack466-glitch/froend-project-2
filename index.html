Apartment Visitor Management

Apartment Visitor Management System is a digital system used to record and monitor visitors entering and leaving an apartment building. It was created to replace manual paper records and make visitor tracking faster, safer, and more organized. This system solves several problems: it improves security by keeping accurate visitor records, reduces human errors from handwritten logs, saves time during check-in and check-out, and helps apartment managers easily track visitor history. Overall, it makes apartment management more efficient, secure, and professional.
